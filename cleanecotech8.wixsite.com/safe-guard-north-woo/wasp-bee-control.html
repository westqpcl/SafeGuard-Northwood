    Wasps Nest Removal methods of Pest control services.

    Baiting

    This is one of the most popular methods of pest control for wasps. It involves placing a trap in area and using attractants such as sugar, honey, etc to lure the wasps.

    Wasp Killer Spray

    Spraying is the most effective way to get rid of wasps. This method involves using a pesticide that is specifically designed to kill wasps.

    Physical Removal

    This method involves physically removing the nests of the wasps. This is not recommended as it can be dangerous and should only be done by a professional pest control service.

    Repellents

    Repellents are also a popular method of wasp control. These products contain natural ingredients that repel wasps and prevent them from returning to the infested area.

    Sticky Traps

    Sticky traps are a type of bait trap that uses an adhesive material to capture wasps. This method is effective for trapping wasps and preventing them from entering the infested area.

    Heat Treatment

    Heat treatment is a non-chemical method of controlling wasps. This involves using heat to kill off the wasps in an infested area.

    Insecticides

    Insecticides are chemical products used to kill off wasps. These products are usually applied in the form of sprays or dusts.

    Electronic Devices

    Electronic devices such as ultrasonic repellers can also be used to control wasps. These devices emit high-frequency sound waves which repel the wasps away from the infested area.
